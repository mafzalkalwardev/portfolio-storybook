Chapter 2 · The Author's Note

About me

Full-Stack Developer & Automation Engineer from Islamabad, Pakistan — building production software for logistics, CRM, email, and AI-powered workflows.

Who I am

I'm Muhammad Afzal Kalwar, a software developer who turns complex business problems into reliable software — auto dialers, CRM platforms, email verification systems, scrapers, and client-facing web apps.

I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from Air University, Islamabad. My degree covered algorithms, data structures, software engineering, operating systems, databases, and computer networks — the foundation I use every day in full-stack and automation work.

I maintain 40+ open source repositories on GitHub, ship Windows desktop tools with PyQt6, build React/Node.js web apps, and automate workflows with Playwright and Selenium. I'm available for freelance, contract, and full-time remote roles worldwide.

Specialties: Google Voice auto dialers · bulk email verification · Fiverr lead CRM · FMCSA carrier scrapers · call analytics · logistics dispatch websites · AI transcription pipelines.
